Tournaments

The tournaments will be completely optional, informal and with no pressure to join.

We want this society to be as inclusive to as many skill levels as possible and whilst competing can be a great way to improve skill, they are often far too competitive for a social society. However, we will be running small tournaments outside of our normal weekly sessions for anybody who wishes to take part.

Any tournaments will be free to enter.

Prizes will be token gifts as to not create undue competitiveness. Yet to be decided.
