  • Tournaments

  • What tournaments do you run?

    Tournaments yet to be decided.

  • Do I have to join the tournaments?

    The tournaments will be completely optional, informal and with no pressure to join.

    We want this society to be as inclusive to as many skill levels as possible and whilst competing can be a great way to improve skill, they are often far too competitive for a social society. However, we will be running small tournaments outside of our normal weekly sessions for anybody who wishes to take part.

  • Is there any entry costs for the tournaments?

    Any tournaments will be free to enter.

  • Is there any prizes for the tournaments?

    Prizes will be token gifts as to not create undue competitiveness. Yet to be decided.

  • Contact Us

  • Who do I contact with any general queries?

    Please message any queries to our instagram account / directly to one of the commiteee on WhatsApp/Instagram and somebody will get back to you as soon as they can.

  • Who do I contact about welfare concerns?

    Our amazing welfare officer Sophie can be contacted either via her email S.Blumenthal2@newcastle.ac.uk or via her instagram (tagged on her committee post).

    Please keep in mine that there is a limit to the support we can provide as a committee. Welfare concerns will be kept confidential unless we feel like there is an active risk to your health / we require further support to help you.

  • Who can read the messages send to the social squash instagram account?

    Many of our current committee have access to read the messages coming into the instagram account.

    The account is not monitored at all times so any urgent concerns should be directed to the appropriate teams associated with the university.
